// what it does

Your buddy, always on screen.

🎭
6 Animated Characters
Wall·E, Pikachu, Luffy, Zoro, Chopper, and Po — each with unique idle, click, celebrate, and 3 right-click personality animations.
✅
Built-in Todo List
Click your buddy to pop open a minimal task panel. Mark things done and watch them celebrate with you.
🖱️
Drag, Rotate, Interact
Drag to move anywhere on screen. Left-drag to rotate your character 360°. Right-click to trigger personality animations.
🚀
Launches at Login
Auto-starts silently when you log in — no dock icon, always floating, always on top of every window.
🦴
Skeletal GLB Animations
Characters use real rigged GLB models from Blender with smooth skeletal animation blending — not just procedural transforms.
🔮
Built for What's Next
Kaaku is the foundation for a full personal AI assistant — voice, memory, integrations, and intelligence coming soon.
